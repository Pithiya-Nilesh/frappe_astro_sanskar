What Information can we collect from you?

  • Identity Data consists of the initial name, maiden name, last name, gender, DOB and other general details.
  • Contact information includes a person's name, address, and phone number.
  • Financial Information comprises bank account and payment card information.
  • Transaction Data consists of Information regarding payments made to and received by you as well as other specifics about the goods and services you have purchased from us.
  • Technical Data contains your IP address, the kind and version of your browser, your location and time zone, the types and versions of your browser's plug-ins, the operating system and platform you're using to access our website, as well as other technical Information about the devices you're using.
  • Profile Data comprises your interests, tastes, input, and survey replies.
  • Usage Data consists of details about how you use our website, products, and services.
  • Marketing and communications data contains your preferences for getting marketing from us and third-party partners, as well as your communication preferences.

How do we use your Information?

We will only use your personal information if it is legal for us to do so. We will most frequently utilize your Personal Information in the following situations:

  • We must fulfill the terms of the agreement we're about to, or have already, made with you.
  • Where we must adhere to a legal requirement.
  • To issue bills and ask for payments for the money you owe us.
  • To manage, troubleshoot, and improve user experience on our website.
  • To assess the effectiveness of a website, including the most popular pages and frequency of visits.
  • To register your Information and sign up for our services.
  • To verify that you have used our services.
  • To get in touch with you and address any questions about our services.
  • To assess your request for a job application.

Security Measures

We are committed to ensuring the security and confidentiality of your Information. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include encryption, regular security audits, access controls, and staff training.
