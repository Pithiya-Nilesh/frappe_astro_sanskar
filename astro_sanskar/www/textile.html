The Out-of-the-Box Features of ERPNext for
the Textile Industry!

ERPNext offers a variety of functionalities to businesses in the textile industry - leading to better automation, streamlined processes, and higher revenues.

Item Variants

Textile manufacturers deal with a wide range of product permutations, such as variations in color, length, or design. In ERPNext, we assist in setting up item templates and variants to manage these complexities efficiently. The primary item is stored as a template, while each specific variation is stored as an item variant.

Maintain a centralized inventory system.

Effective tracking and managing the different product options available.

Easy updates and navigation.

Shop Floor Management

Efficient shop floor management is crucial for textile manufacturers to maintain smooth operations and optimize production processes. Through our implementation of ERPNext, we configure features such as Job Cards, Operations, and Workstations.

Automate repetitive activities on the shop floor.

Real-time visibility into job work status, workstation locations, employee assignments, and progress.

Easy monitoring of the status and next steps for each work order.

Material Resource Planning (MRP)

The procurement and production planning for textile products often involves dealing with a vast number of product variants, which can quickly become complicated. Our implementation solutions for ERPNext streamline the MRP process for textile companies.

Managing work orders against sales or handling material requests.

You can track the material consumption of batched inventory.

Curbs unnecessary expenses and achieves zero wastage of resources.

Efficient material planning and optimizing your production processes.

Capacity Planning

Optimizing workstations and eliminating bottlenecks is essential for effective production management in textile manufacturing. Through ERPNext's capacity planning features, we help textile companies optimize their workstations based on available resources and production orders.

Creating a capacity plan to strategically allocate resources, manage workloads, and ensure smooth operations.

Maintain and manage planning schedules for better production planning and resource utilization.

Identify and address capacity constraints, improving overall efficiency and productivity.

Multiple Units of Measurement

Textile manufacturers often have specific measurement requirements for their inventory, which may involve multiple units of measurement. In ERPNext, we assist in configuring the system to accommodate different units of measurement for your inventory items.

Seamlessly handle conversions between units.

Facilitates smooth sales, purchase, and stock transactions.

Accurate measurement tracking and simplifies inventory management.

Multilingual Textile Manufacturing ERP

To cater to a diverse user base and create a welcoming environment, multilingual support is crucial in a textile manufacturing ERP. With ERPNext, we implement the preferred language localizations to support over 100 languages.

Users can access and use the system in their preferred language.

You can even get it customized on your preferred language(If not available).

These features ensure that language barriers do not hinder your textile business.
