General Overview!

GramDharaa Gaushala owns several gaushala stores, selling cow-based products across India. They have started NETTYAM, an initiative of Gramdharaa Gaushala towards a healthy lifestyle by selling eco-friendly and panchgavya products.

Struggling to keep both gaushala and stores in sync, along with inefficient inventory and logistics management - they reached out to Sanskar Technolab for end-to-end management(right from manufacturing, and inventory to sales and CRM).

Why This Special Project For Sanskar Technolab?

We didn't charge any money for this work!

It's part of our special CSR(Corporate Social Responsibility) program to help our peers and make a good impact in what we do best(technology and ERPNext).

The ERPNext Modules We Have Implemented For GramDharaa Gaushala!

Purchase Module

In addressing GramDharaa Gaushala's procurement challenges, our enhancements in the Purchase Module have significantly streamlined their purchasing processes.

  • Purchase order & invoice management: Introduced a custom field in the item child table, for precise item selection based on company and item code, for purchase orders and invoice management.
  • Customized print format: Implemented a tailored print format to ensure that documentation aligns seamlessly with the organization's requirements.
  • Email configurations for suppliers: The establishment of email configurations for better supplier correspondence & supplier relationship management.
  • GST purchase management: With the ERPNext Purchase module, our client can seamlessly handle GST intricacies, for tax management between loose and packaged products.
Manufacturing Module

In revolutionizing GramDharaa Gaushala's manufacturing processes, our interventions in the Manufacturing Module have been instrumental. We have tailored the solutions to create a cohesive manufacturing ecosystem.

  • Bill of materials: We have set up and customized the Bill of Material (BOM) to ensure accurate product assembly.
  • Customized production plan: Configuring and customizing the Production Plan and Operation modules for comprehensive production planning and operational efficiency.
  • Workstation setup: Our team has helped them with workstation setup and configuration to streamline the manufacturing workflow.
  • Production control: The introduction of work orders and Job Cards, coupled with routing configurations, for better production control and management.
Inventory Module

Our implementations in the Inventory Module have brought about a paradigm shift in Gram Dhara Gaushala's stock management.

  • Multi-location management: Configured the Warehouse Master for multi-location stock tracking across different locations.
  • Batch-wise tracking: The introduction of batch-wise product definitions in the Item Master enhances control and precise inventory management.
  • Easy re-ordering: Re-order level configurations optimize the replenishment process, guaranteeing timely restocking of essential items.
  • Enhanced categorization: Development of custom fields for item templates for specific item variants selection, for streamlined categorization.
Sales Module

Our enhancements in the Sales Module have been pivotal in transforming GramDharaa Gaushala's sales operations.

  • Sales Order & Invoice Management: Implemented custom fields in the item child table for precise item selection based on company and item code in the Sales Order and Invoice modules.
  • Customized print formats: The customization of print formats to ensure precise sales documentation.
  • Better leads and opportunities management: GramDharaa Gaushala can better manage leads, contacts, and opportunities effectively
CRM Module

Our team at Sanskar Technolab has also implemented the ERPNext CRM module for Gram Dhara Gaushala to improve their customer-centric operations and boost customer satisfaction.

  • Master setup: We have set up the Customer Master, Address and Contact Master, Territory Master, and Brand Master for better management of customer data, contacts, and brand relationships.
  • End-to-End Customer data management: The configuration of the CRM module enables effective lead management and opportunity tracking.
  • Analytics for the win: ERPNext CRM modules have reports and analytics tools that offer valuable insights to make data-driven decisions and enhance overall business performance.
POS Module

In revolutionizing GramDharaa Gaushala's payments and retail experience as a whole, we have set up a POS module for them. By creating, updating, and deleting different POS profiles, we've tailored the system to the organization's specific needs.

  • Managing payments: The configuration of POS profiles includes managing modes of payment, opening entries, and closing entries for smooth retail transactions.
  • Advanced features implementation: We've introduced features like managing POS shifts, handling returns and refunds, and even advance payments.
  • Branch-wise management: Configured branch-wise POS profiles for versatile store management across different areas.

Their Challenges & Our Solutions(Leveraging ERPNext)

Challenge Solution

Inefficiencies in stock tracking and management

The existing custom software struggled to effectively handle stock, leading to inefficiencies in tracking and managing inventory levels. ERPNext brought an integrated approach to inventory management, providing a unified platform for seamless tracking and efficient management of inventory throughout the manufacturing cycle.

Sales and Accounting Hassles

Sales and accounting issues are also piling up, with the current software falling short of providing a comprehensive solution. The lack of integration between the ledger, accounting, and inventory caused disruptions in the financial workflow. Sanskar Technolab completed the integration of ledger, accounting, and inventory within ERPNext to eliminate the challenges in the sales and accounting departments. This comprehensive approach streamlined financial processes and ensured accuracy.

Manufacturing and Packaging Disconnect

The manufacturing process in the gaushala and subsequent packaging in the store for selling lacked synchronization. This disjointed approach hindered the seamless flow of products from production to the store shelves, leading to inefficient tracking and management of operations. ERPNext bridged the gap between gaushala manufacturing and store packaging, ensuring a smooth transition of products from production to store shelves. This synchronization enhanced the efficiency of the entire manufacturing cycle.

GST Complexity

In the textile industry, tracking gray inward, its processing, and costing becomes difficult, which also increases the total time spent throughout the entire process. The ERPNext system was configured to handle GST intricacies, accurately distinguishing between loose products and packaged store products for end-to-end management.

Missing CRM and POS

The absence of Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Point of Sale (POS) systems hindered customer management and payments. This gap in technology impacted the overall customer experience and the efficiency of payment processes. ERPNext introduced CRM and POS modules, addressing the lack of customer management and payment efficiency. It leads to a better overall customer experience, allowing for better communication and streamlined payment processes.
